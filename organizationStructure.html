Our Management

The company structure of Meshack PM Enterprise is comprised of different roles which include the Group CEO, Administrator, Developers, Practitioners, a Data Scientist, Strategy and Marketing practitioner, Legal practitioner and Accountant

Group CEO is the representative of the company, he strings the different roles of developers, practitioners and scientists together to ensure that the company achieves its bottom-line of producing competitive web products and services on time and backed highest standards of customer care.

Administrator is responsible for ensuring that all books are put together and up to date. The administrator is responsible for ensuring that Meshack PM Enterprise maintains healthy and continuous two-way communication with its clients.

Accountant is ensures that financial books are balanced and reflect the company's growth targets, is responsible for tax affairs with SARS and CSD.

Legal Practitioner is responsible for drafting legal documents for HR and client and partnerhips relations, to protect the best interests of the company.

Strategy and Marketing practitioner is responsible for business strategy and brand development.
