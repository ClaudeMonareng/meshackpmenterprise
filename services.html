What is Blockchain tokenization?

Blockchain Tokenization is a process of converting value of real world assets into digital tokens, that can be transferable between buyers and sellers, on exchange platforms or person-to-person.

We tokenize real world assets into digital tokens for our clients, using NFT protocols, such as ERC20. We are also looking to explore building our in-house exchange platform for these tokens, so that our clients can their assets quicker and with ease.
