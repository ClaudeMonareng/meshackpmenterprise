How Our Business Operates

Website development involves different levels or stages before a web product can be fully consumed by a user. We are involved in every level of web development. We build a website and/or web application for our client. We purchase a website domain on behalf of the client from a domain registrar and integrate it with the website. We update and maintain content and security of the client's website on an annual basis.
